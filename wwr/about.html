DRY OAR

purpose

At DRY OAR, our purpose is to provide unforgettable rafting experiences that connect people with nature and promote adventure in a safe and responsible way. We believe in the power of teamwork, respect for the environment, and the joy of exploring rivers together. Our goal is to create lasting memories for our clients while fostering a love for the great outdoors.

Our History

Dry Oar was founded in 2002 by a group of young enthusiasts who all their desire was to connect with nature. They started with a small group of friends and a single raft, exploring the rivers of Mexico. Over the years, their passion for adventure and commitment to safety attracted more people, leading to the establishment of Dry Oar as a professional rafting company.

Our commitment is to provide happiness and joy to our country and all the world. Dry Oar always strives to offer the best rafting experiences, making a unique connection with nature and the rivers we navigate.

Don't lose this opportunity and connect with your loved ones and nature. Join us and be part of this incredible adventure!
